ABOUT US

Garden Mania Story is a captivating match-3 puzzle game where players solve puzzles and grow their own virtual gardens. The game offers hundreds of fun and challenging levels, with seasonal updates and special events to keep the game fresh. Suitable for players of all ages, Garden Mania Story combines puzzle-solving with a charming gardening experience.
